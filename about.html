About Us

★ This platform helps students of Jeppiaar Institute of Technology access and download handwritten notes for each paper, every semester.

★ It is designed to make studying easier and more organized by providing all notes in one place, categorized by subjects and units.

★ Students can DOWNLOAD notes easily, making it convenient to study anytime, anywhere.

★ If a student has efficient or useful notes, they can also upload and share them by creating an account, helping other students.

★ By centralizing all handwritten notes digitally, this platform enhances learning efficiency and convenience for every student.
